Experience Toll Road Demand

VLC has extensive specialist experience in the forecasting of traffic demands on roads with tolls, having developed state-of-the-art toll modelling algorithms and procedures. These algorithms put us at the forefront of travel demand forecasting on toll roads, with our toll modelling capabilities far surpassing anything seen before in Australia. This has led to major involvement in many toll road projects  over recent years. VLC has also been approached on a number of occasions to perform audits and peer reviews of toll road forecasts.

Total Choice

Zenith models are each fitted with a sophisticated traffic assignment algorithm that uses travel market segmentation to determine the likelihood of different segments to use a tolled route. This algorithm allows the presence of tolls to influence the choice of route not only in terms of competition between tolled and untolled roads, but between tolled roads as well. This feature is becoming increasingly critical as the scale of the toll road network expands in Australia’s east coast capital cities.
